Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What oil should I use for my Toyota Vitz?

For most Toyota Vitz models (2005+), we recommend 5W-30 full synthetic oil. For older models or high-mileage engines, 10W-40 semi-synthetic works well. Use our Oil Finder tool on the homepage for a precise recommendation.

Do you offer mobile oil change services?

Yes! Our mobile service units operate throughout Addis Ababa. We can perform oil changes at your home, office, or any location. Service includes oil, filter replacement, and basic vehicle check. Call +251 933 680 059 to schedule.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, Telebirr, CBE Birr, bank transfers, and cash on delivery (for orders under ETB 5,000). Corporate clients can apply for 30-day credit terms with approved application.

How quickly can you deliver?

Within Addis Ababa: Same-day delivery for orders placed before 3 PM. Next-day for orders after 3 PM. Outside Addis: 2-3 business days to major cities (Adama, Hawassa, Bahir Dar, Dire Dawa).

Are your products genuine?

Absolutely. We are authorized distributors for TotalEnergies, Shell, Castrol, and Mobil 1. Every product comes with batch numbers and can be verified with manufacturers. We provide SDS, TDS, and COA documents for all products.
