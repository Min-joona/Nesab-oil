Our Mission

To provide Ethiopia's vehicle owners and fleet operators with world-class lubrication solutions that maximize performance, minimize downtime, and reduce operational costs through technical expertise and reliable service.

We believe that every engine deserves the best protection, whether it's a single motorcycle or a fleet of heavy-duty trucks. Our mission is to democratize access to premium lubricants and expert knowledge across Ethiopia.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's most trusted lubrication partner, recognized for technical excellence, environmental responsibility, and transformative impact on vehicle maintenance standards.

By 2030, we aim to serve over 1 million vehicles annually, operate 10 distribution centers across Ethiopia, and establish a state-of-the-art oil analysis laboratory in Addis Ababa.

Our Journey

From startup to industry leader in the future

2018 - Foundation

Addis Ababa

Nesab was founded with a simple goal: to address the lack of quality lubricants and technical expertise in Ethiopia's automotive sector. Started with 5 firends and a small warehouse in Lafto.

Future Plans

Future

2019 - First Major Fleet Contract

Milestone

To Secure our first major fleet contract with a public transportation company, providing lubricants for 150 buses. This will validate our business model and quality standards.

2021 - Expansion & Certification

Growth

To Achieve API certification for our product line and expanded to a 2,000 sqm warehouse. And to Launch technical consultation services.

2023 - Digital Transformation

Innovation

Launch e-commerce platform, real-time inventory system, and GPS-tracked delivery fleet. And then to serve over 500 fleet clients and process 1,000+ orders monthly.

2024 - Leadership Position

Leadership

Market leader in Addis Ababa with 45% fleet market share. To be recognized by Ethiopian Petroleum Association for excellence in distribution and customer service.

Our Facilities

Modern infrastructure ensuring quality and efficiency

Distribution Center

Our 2,000 square meter facility in Lafto is equipped with climate-controlled storage, automated dispensing systems, and a capacity of 500,000 liters. Strict FIFO inventory management ensures fresh products always.

Quality Control Lab

On-site laboratory for quality verification, viscosity testing, and contamination analysis. Every batch is tested before distribution, ensuring 100% compliance with specifications.
