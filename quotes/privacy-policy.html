Nesab Vehicle Oil Company ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website nesab-oil.com or make purchases from us.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Company name and business details
  • Delivery addresses
  • Payment information (processed securely; we don't store full card details)
  • Vehicle information and service requirements

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Pages visited, time spent, navigation paths
  • Location data (approximate region, not precise GPS)
  • Cookies and tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Provide technical consultation and support
  • Send order confirmations, invoices, and delivery updates
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer service
  • Improve our products, services, and website
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For customers in the EU/EEA, we process your data based on:

  • Contract: To fulfill your orders
  • Consent: For marketing communications
  • Legitimate Interest: For fraud prevention and business improvement
  • Legal Obligation: For tax and accounting records

4. Data Sharing

We may share your data with:

  • Delivery Partners: To fulfill deliveries (name, address, phone)
  • Payment Processors: To process payments securely
  • Service Providers: IT support, analytics, marketing tools
  • Legal Authorities: When required by Ethiopian law

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Order Information: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Account Data: Until you delete your account
  • Marketing Data: Until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics Data: 26 months (Google Analytics default)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion (where legally possible)
  • Object to processing or marketing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at:

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Remember your login session
  • Store cart contents
  • Track website usage (analytics)
  • Personalize content and ads

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

  • SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Access controls and authentication

9. International Transfers

Your data is stored on servers located in Germany (Hetzner) and accessed only by authorized personnel in Ethiopia. We ensure adequate protection per GDPR requirements.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect children's data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes take effect immediately upon posting. Material changes will be notified via email or banner.
