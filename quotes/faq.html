Product Questions

The correct viscosity depends on your vehicle's make, model, year, and operating conditions:

  • Modern petrol engines (2010+): Typically 5W-30 or 0W-20 for fuel efficiency
  • Diesel engines: Commonly 15W-40 or 10W-40, especially for commercial vehicles
  • Heavy-duty trucks: Usually 20W-50 or 15W-40 for high-temperature protection
  • High-performance vehicles: Synthetic 5W-40 or 10W-60

Always check your owner's manual first. For personalized recommendations, use our Oil Selector Tool or call our technical hotline at +251 93 368 0059.

Our lubricants meet or exceed major OEM specifications:

  • Toyota/Lexus: Approved for Toyota TGMO requirements
  • Mercedes-Benz: MB-Approval 229.5/229.3 compliant
  • BMW: LL-01/LL-04 specifications
  • Volkswagen: 502.00/505.00 and 508.00/509.00 standards
  • Ford: WSS-M2C913-C/D and WSS-M2C948-B

Each product page includes a detailed compatibility matrix. For older vehicles or specific models, contact our technical team with your VIN number for exact recommendations.

When stored properly (indoors, 15-25°C, sealed containers):

  • Engine oils (unopened): 3-5 years from manufacture date
  • Transmission fluids: 5 years
  • Gear oils: 5-7 years
  • Grease: 2-3 years (check for oil separation)
  • Coolants: 3 years (sealed), 1 year (opened)

Check the batch code on the container - format: DDMMYY-XXXX. We guarantee minimum 18 months remaining shelf life for all products sold. Proper storage extends shelf life significantly. Avoid direct sunlight and temperature extremes.

Documents are available in English and Amharic. SDS sheets include handling, storage, disposal, and first aid information. TDS sheets provide detailed performance specifications, test results, and OEM approvals.
